about me

hey, i'm diego cardenas.

i am a first generation peruvian-american living in houston, texas. my dream right now is to go to ut austin and major in computer engineering. computers have always been a huge passion for me, and i've always made an effort to develop my knowledge and technological skills, whether that be through computer science classes at school, finding new software to learn, or generally experimenting and tinkering around. in my free time, i love to play video games, hang out with my friends, and learn more about my interests.

hobbies
